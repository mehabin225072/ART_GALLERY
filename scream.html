The Scream

Artist: Edvard Munch
Year of Creation: 1893

The Scream is one of the most recognizable paintings in the world. Created by Norwegian artist Edvard Munch, it symbolizes fear, anxiety, and emotional distress. The dramatic sky and expressive figure make it a masterpiece of Expressionism.
