Girl with a Pearl Earring

Artist: Johannes Vermeer
Year of Creation: 1665

Girl with a Pearl Earring is one of Johannes Vermeer's most celebrated paintings. Often called the "Dutch Mona Lisa," it is admired for the girl's captivating expression, beautiful lighting, and the striking pearl earring.
