Mona Lisa

Artist: Leonardo da Vinci
Year of Creation: 1503

The Mona Lisa is one of the most famous paintings in the world. Created by Leonardo da Vinci during the Renaissance, it is admired for the subject's mysterious smile, lifelike details, and artistic brilliance. Today, it is displayed in the Louvre Museum in Paris.
