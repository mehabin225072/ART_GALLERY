The Persistence of Memory

Artist: Salvador Dalí
Year of Creation: 1931

The Persistence of Memory is one of Salvador Dalí's most famous surrealist paintings. It is known for its melting clocks, dreamlike atmosphere, and symbolic representation of the fluid nature of time.
